About us:

Solve Product Design has been providing mechanical design and Engineering services, primarily in Winnipeg, since 2008. We're a small firm, but we pride ourselves on providing high quality and personal service. Our low overhead costs allow us to provide our services at very competitive rates.

Blaine McFarlane, P.Eng. | President

Blaine is a Mechanical Engineer with 20 years experience in new product development. He founded Solve Product Design in 2008. Blaine is a runner, kiteboarder, skier (water, down-hill, & cross-country) and woodworker. He also enjoys home renovation, landscaping and reading. He has been volunteering his time on the Urbee , low-energy car project since 2008. Blaine lives in Winnipeg with his wife and two children.
